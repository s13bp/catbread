    Catbread is a retro P2E arcade powered by NFTs. Simplicity and nostalgia meet the digital revolution to create a project that’s not only fun, but lucrative.






    OUR VISION





    Catbread aims to be the go to for players interested in casual gaming that focuses on gameplay and community above all else. To do this, we’re creating an arcade that integrates upgradeable NFTs, leveling, weekly competitions, staking, and an ever expanding collection of virtual arcade cabinets.

    The only “idle” earning you’ll find here is in staking. You can stake your NFTs and your tokens alike. Gain NFTs at a bargain for locking up tokens for a set amount of time, or earn Catbread from classic token staking. You can even earn BUSD via a portion of the royalties from NFT sales by staking your unused NFTs.

    TEAM PROMISE





    There aren’t any fat dev wallets here. The team has a publicly available wallet with 7% - that’s 1% per core team member locked for six months and paid out over another two after that. Catbread’s philosophy has always been to make it with our community, not to feed off of them like many of the projects out there. We earn our funding for marketing and development just as our holders do - through the tax on token transactions and the royalties from NFTs.

    We promise to uplift not just our community, but the BSC NFT community as a whole via the regular commissioning of artists voted on by the community. These artists will be paid to create Catbread themed NFTs that will be featured as 1/1 pieces winnable in the arcade, or won via staking.

    We promise no idle games, no guff, no sacrificing gameplay, no shoehorned NFTs - just an ode to classic gaming, the admiration of art, and an ecosystem that makes our holders money while doing it.
    Come make it with us and have a blast while doing it.
